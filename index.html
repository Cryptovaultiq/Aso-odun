How it Works

Select Your Wallet

We have varieties of wallets you can choose from to validate or synchronize your wallet. Please select your correct wallet name before proceeding.

Validate Your Wallet

Once you select your wallet, you will be prompted to connect via a decentralized protocol, This can successfully be completed manually or automatically.

Generate QR Code

Upon Successful, a QR code will be generated for your wallet. You can always scan your QR code to reinitialize your wallet.

Save Wallet QR Code

Save your generated mnemonic code or QR code is important. You can click on save or screenshot the QR code page, You choose how you want to Save.

Get the latest news and updates about wallet features and protocol upgrades.
